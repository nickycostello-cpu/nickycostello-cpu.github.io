A marketing leader who works at the intersection of strategy, storytelling and performance across SaaS, film, education and commercial services.

Commercial Print · SEO, Content Architecture & Digital Strategy

McGowans Print

The Challenge

Increase digital visibility and organic search performance in a competitive commercial print market across Ireland and the UK, while building a content architecture to support the wider McGowans Group strategy.

I delivered a comprehensive technical SEO and content architecture overhaul — reviewing site structure, improving internal linking, and writing all technology and blog content. I developed a keyword and metadata strategy targeting high-value service and location searches, and provided strategic direction for the broader McGowans Group project to ensure consistency and commercial impact across the portfolio. The platform was also prepared for Phase 2 conversion optimisation and HubSpot CRM integration to enable scalable lead attribution.

  • Developed strategic content architecture aligning SEO, technical positioning and commercial growth across the Group portfolio
  • Organic impressions grew from 41.8K to 77.8K — an 86% increase
  • Clicks increased by 15% through targeted content improvements
  • Average search ranking improved from 18.5 to 14
  • Dublin clicks nearly doubled; sustained growth across Belfast and UK searches
  • All website technology and blog content written and structured for SEO performance
  • Platform prepared for Phase 2 HubSpot CRM integration and lead attribution

Hybrid Mail Solutions

The Challenge

Hybrid Mail Solutions needed a complete brand identity created from the ground up — alongside a clearer digital presence and a website that properly communicated the value of its end-to-end print and post services to clients across Ireland and Northern Ireland.

I created the full brand identity for Hybrid Mail Solutions — developing visual direction, tone of voice, naming framework and all website content from scratch. The project included a complete information architecture redesign for clearer user journeys, new service and product messaging, and a scalable content framework to support future campaigns and business growth. Every touchpoint, from homepage to service pages, was written and structured with both audience clarity and long-term brand consistency in mind.
