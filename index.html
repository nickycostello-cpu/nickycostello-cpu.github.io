McGowans Group · Brand Architecture, Strategy & Full Digital Build

McGowans Group

The Challenge

What began as a website refresh evolved into a full strategic repositioning — creating McGowans Group as a parent brand unifying four subsidiaries: McGowans Print, BoxJob, Hybrid Mail Solutions and Goohme (3D out of home and experiential OOH), spanning commercial print, eCommerce, hybrid mail and outdoor advertising.

This is an ongoing, multi-layered engagement spanning brand strategy, digital architecture and content, operating closer to embedded CMO than consulting retainer. From strategic foundations through to developer briefs and live content, every element of the Group's digital presence has been shaped, written or directed through this project.

Brand & Strategy

  • Full McGowans Group parent brand created: masterbrand architecture, tagline, visual system and 11 product colour ramps
  • Brand system delivered in four formats: interactive HTML, PowerPoint, Word style guide and CSS/SCSS design tokens
  • CEO presentation, C-Suite decision document and Brand Direction paper produced for board-level sign-off
  • Naming convention strategy developed to preserve McGowans Print SEO equity while establishing the Group wrapper

Website & Digital Architecture

  • Full website architecture designed across 11 service lines, 3 subsidiary pages, portfolio, insights, sustainability and contact
  • 9-section product page master template with hero, interactive calculator, FAQPage JSON-LD schema, downloads and CTA band
  • Three-layer search strategy: SEO, AEO via FAQPage schema, and GEO for AI search citations
  • Developer Guide, Site Architecture brief and 33-amendment consolidation document produced for the build team

Content, SEO & Social

  • Organic impressions grew from 41.8K to 77.8K, an 86% increase, with average ranking improving from 18.5 to 14
  • All blog, guide and download content written for McGowans Print and Hybrid Mail Solutions from August 2025
  • Interactive ROI calculator built and embedded for the Christmas Packaging & POS campaign
  • Ongoing social media strategy, copy and content management across McGowans and HMS accounts

Hybrid Mail Solutions

The Challenge

Hybrid Mail Solutions needed a complete brand identity created from the ground up, alongside a clearer digital presence and a website that properly communicated the value of its end-to-end print and post services to clients across Ireland and Northern Ireland.

I created the full brand identity for Hybrid Mail Solutions, developing visual direction, tone of voice, naming framework and all website content from scratch. The project included a complete information architecture redesign for clearer user journeys, new service and product messaging, and a scalable content framework to support future campaigns and business growth. Every touchpoint, from homepage to service pages, was written and structured with both audience clarity and long-term brand consistency in mind.

  • Full brand identity created from the ground up: visual direction, tone of voice and messaging framework
  • Complete website content written with new service and product messaging
  • Site architecture redesigned for clearer, conversion-focused user journeys
  • Consistent brand voice established across all digital touchpoints
  • Scalable content structure built for future marketing activity and campaigns
